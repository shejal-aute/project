Welcome to Remine India!

We are a recognized startup dedicated to recycling e-waste and Li batteries. Our mission is to create a cleaner and healthier environment by reducing electronic waste and its harmful impact on the planet.

We offer a range of services including e-waste pickup and recycling, safe and efficient disposal of Li batteries, and secure data destruction. We also work with businesses and organizations to help them properly dispose of their electronic waste and batteries.
